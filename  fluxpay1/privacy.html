Privacy Policy

Your data is safe with us. Learn how we protect your information.

Last Updated: 23 June 2026

1 Introduction

At Flux Pay, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

By using Flux Pay, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

Our Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services.

3 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process your earnings and withdrawals
  • To verify your identity (KYC compliance)
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • To send important notifications about your account
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Hosted on cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime
  • Access Control: Strict internal access controls and audit logs
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments performed quarterly
  • Firewalls: Advanced network protection against attacks

Bank-Level Security: Your bank details are encrypted using AES-256 encryption — the same standard used by banks worldwide.

5 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Payment Processors: To process withdrawals to your bank
  • Verification Partners: To confirm your identity (KYC)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by Nigerian law
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics (anonymized data only)

We Never Share: Your password, full bank details, or ID documents with marketing companies or unrelated third parties.

6 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in during your session
  • Remember your preferences (dark mode, language)
  • Analyze platform usage to improve features
  • Detect suspicious activity and prevent fraud

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7 Your Rights

As a Flux Pay user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Objection: Opt out of non-essential communications

Contact us at support@fluxpay.com to exercise any of these rights. We respond within 30 days.

8 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data removed within 90 days of deletion request
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer for compliance
  • Transaction Records: Kept for 7 years per Nigerian financial regulations

9 Children's Privacy

Flux Pay is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a user is under 18, we will delete their account and data immediately.

Parents/Guardians: If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately at support@fluxpay.com.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • In-app notification banner
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

Continued use of Flux Pay after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
